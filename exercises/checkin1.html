Gluten Free Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

I grew up with a house full of baked goods. My mom started a baking company when I was about six years old, so I naturally became her in-house pastry tester. As an avid cookie enthusiast with a credible palate, here is my go-to oatmeal chocolate chip cookie recipe;

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons of unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup gluten free quick cook oats
  • 1 cup gluten free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups Enjoy Life Gluten free choloate chips

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Line several cookie sheets with parchment paper, or lightly grease with non-stick vegetable oil spray.

Step 2

Beat together the butter and sugars until smooth.

Step 3

Add the egg and vanilla, beating to combine.

Step 4

Whisk together the flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda, xanthan gum, and salt, and add to the butter mixture in the bowl.

Step 5

Mix until everything is thoroughly incorporated. Scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl, and mix briefly.

Step 6

Stir in the chocolate chips.

Step 7

Scoop the dough by the teaspoonful onto the prepared baking sheets; a teaspoon cookie scoop works well here. Leave about 1 1/2" between cookies.

Step 8

Bake the cookies for 13 to 14 minutes, until they're a light golden brown, with slightly darker edges. Their middles may still look a tiny bit shiny; that's OK, they'll continue to bake as they cool on the pan.

Step 9

Remove the cookies from the oven, and as soon as they're set enough to handle, transfer them to racks to cool.

Special Tips

  1. You can make adjustments where you feel you need to. Make them your own!
  2. You can double or half the recipe to make as many or as little cookies as you'd like.
  3. Enjoy them warm with a cup of cold milk!
